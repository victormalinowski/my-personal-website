About Me

Hi, I'm Victor! I am an undergrad student at the University of Waterloo currently majoring in biostatistics. My main area of study consists of probability theory, statistical inference, and forecasting techniques; however, I have a broad range of other interests both inside and outside of mathematics.

Early in my undergraduate career, I had the opportunity to get acquainted with R Markdown during an introductory data analysis course. From there I began exploring other markup languages such as LaTeX and HTML, which led to the creation of this website. In the future, I plan to learn more about real analysis and stochastic processes.

When not studying math, I like learning about history, geography, and astronomy. I also enjoy hiking, cycling, and watching sports. You may wish to view my current resumé.
